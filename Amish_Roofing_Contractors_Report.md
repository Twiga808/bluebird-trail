## Recommendations for Obtaining Quotes

When contacting these contractors for a quote at your property on Creek Rd, consider the following:

1. **Response Times:** Many Amish contractors do not use email or the internet extensively. Phone calls are the most reliable method of contact. You may need to leave a voicemail and wait for a return call, as they are often on job sites during the day.
2. **Estimates:** Most of the contractors listed offer free estimates. It is advisable to obtain at least three quotes to compare pricing and proposed materials.
3. **Warranties:** Pay close attention to the warranties offered. For example, Fisher's Roofing offers a 10-year workmanship warranty, while Elmer's Roofing offers a 7-year warranty with a no-mess guarantee.
4. **Clean-up:** Amish roofers are generally renowned for their meticulous clean-up. Companies like B&E Roofing even use specialized equipment (the "Equipter") to ensure no debris is left on your property.
