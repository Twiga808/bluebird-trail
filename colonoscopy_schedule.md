## Daily Schedule: Sunday, March 22 to Wednesday, March 25

The following table outlines the specific actions required each day leading up to your procedure. It is critical to follow these instructions exactly to ensure a successful clean-out.

| Date & Time | Action Required | Details |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sunday, March 22 (Tonight)** | **Review Medications & Diet** | Ensure you have stopped taking any medications containing iron (required 5 days prior). Continue all other prescribed medications, including aspirin and Plavix, unless instructed otherwise. Continue to strictly avoid all high-fiber foods (seeds, whole grains, corn, nuts, beans, peels, oatmeal, peas). Drink at least eight 8-ounce glasses of fluids daily. |
| **Monday, March 23** | **Maintain Diet & Hydration** | Continue to strictly avoid all high-fiber foods. Continue drinking at least eight 8-ounce glasses of fluids throughout the day. Continue your regular medications (excluding iron supplements). |
| **Tuesday, March 24 (Before 9:00 AM)** | **Final Solid Meal** | You may eat a fiber-free breakfast. Acceptable items include 2 fried or boiled eggs, ½ cup milk, ½ cup yogurt (no red/purple coloring, no fruit pieces), up to 2 slices of cheese, 2-3 slices of white bread, and 1 tablespoon of butter or olive oil. |
| **Tuesday, March 24 (After 12:00 Noon)** | **Begin Clear Liquid Diet** | **STOP ALL SOLID FOOD.** Begin a strict clear liquid diet. Acceptable liquids include water, black coffee or tea (no milk/cream), clear juices (apple, white grape, lemonade without pulp), broth/bouillon, Gatorade, Kool-Aid, Crystal Light, carbonated soft drinks (Coke, Sprite), and Jell-O. Do not consume red or purple liquids, juice with pulp, dairy, or alcohol. |
| **Tuesday, March 24 (2:00 PM)** | **Initial Medication & Prep Mix** | Take the four 5mg Dulcolax (bisacodyl) tablets with 8 oz of water. Mix the entire 238g bottle of MiraLAX into 64 oz of your chosen clear liquid in a pitcher. You may chill this mixture in the refrigerator, as drinking it cold through a straw can improve tolerance. |
| **Tuesday, March 24 (4:00 PM - 6:00 PM)** | **First Dose of Liquid Prep** | Begin drinking the MiraLAX mixture. Drink one 8 oz glass every 20 minutes for 2 hours and 40 minutes until the 64 oz is finished. While drinking the prep, also consume an additional four to eight 8 oz glasses of clear liquid to prevent dehydration. If you experience nausea, take a 30 to 45-minute break before resuming. |
| **Tuesday, March 24 (8:00 PM)** | **Cannabis Restriction & Check** | If you smoke or vaporize cannabis, you must cease all use 12 hours prior to the procedure (by 8:30 PM). If there is still no success by 9:00 PM - 10:00 PM, call the office. |
| **Tuesday, March 24 (9:00 PM)** | **Evening Medication** | Take two 125mg Simethicone (Gas-X) tablets. Continue drinking clear liquids to remain hydrated. |
| **Wednesday, March 25 (4:30 AM)** | **Final Prep Dose & Strict Cutoff** | Pour the 10 oz bottle of Magnesium Citrate over ice and drink it within 15 minutes. Immediately after finishing, take the remaining two 125mg Simethicone (Gas-X) tablets. Take any necessary morning medications at this time with a sip of water (Diabetics: Do not take your usual oral diabetes medications this morning). **STOP DRINKING ALL FLUIDS.** All prep and clear liquids must be completed exactly 4 hours prior to your appointment. Consuming any fluids after this point may result in your procedure being rescheduled. No hard candy or chewing gum is allowed. |
| **Wednesday, March 25 (7:00 AM)** | **Final Status Check** | Your stool should be clear/light yellow (like weak lemonade). If it is opaque brown/orange with particles, you are not ready. If you are not clear by this time, call the endoscopy center. |
| **Wednesday, March 25 (8:30 AM)** | **Arrival at Center** | Arrive at the Mid Atlantic Endoscopy Center. You must be accompanied by a responsible adult who will stay with you and drive you home. Bus, taxi, shuttle, or rideshare drivers are not permitted to serve as your responsible adult. Wear comfortable clothing and leave all valuables and jewelry (including body piercings) at home. |

## Post-Procedure Instructions

Following the procedure, you must rest and relax for the remainder of the day. Your responsible adult should stay with you for 24 hours. You may resume your normal diet slowly as tolerated. Do not drive, operate machinery, sign legal documents, or make critical decisions until the next day. Furthermore, do not consume alcohol or take unprescribed medications for 24 hours. Mild abdominal discomfort or a feeling of fullness from carbon dioxide is normal; walking can help expel excess gas.
